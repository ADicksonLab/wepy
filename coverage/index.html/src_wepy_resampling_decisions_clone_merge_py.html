39class MultiCloneMergeDecision(Decision): 

40 """Decision encoding cloning and merging decisions for weighted ensemble. 

41 

42 The decision records have in addition to the 'decision_id' a field 

43 called 'target_idxs'. This field has differing interpretations 

44 depending on the 'decision_id'. 

45 

46 For NOTHING and KEEP_MERGE it indicates the walker index to assign 

47 this sample to after resampling. In this sense the walker is 

48 merely a vessel for the propagation of the state and acts as a 

49 slot. 

50 

51 For SQUASH it indicates the walker that it's weight will be given 

52 to, which must have a KEEP_MERGE record for it. 

53 

54 For CLONE it indicates the walker indices that clones of this one 

55 will be placed in. This field is variable length and the length 

56 corresponds to the number of clones. 

57 

58 """
