General Information
===================

Bugs, issues, and feature requests
----------------------------------

-  `Issue Tracker <https://github.com/ADicksonLab/wepy/issues>`__

Builds, Distributions, and Packages
-----------------------------------

``wepy`` releases and packages are available on:

-  `PyPI <https://pypi.org/project/wepy/>`__
-  `Github Releases <https://github.com/ADicksonLab/wepy/releases>`__

There are also historical releases archived on
`Zenodo <https://zenodo.org/badge/latestdoi/101077926>`__ that also are
assigned DOIs for easy citations.

DOI: `10.5281/zenodo.3973431 <https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.3973431>`__

Community & Related Projects
----------------------------

General chat and discussion takes place on
`gitter.im <https://gitter.im/wepy/general>`__.

We will also list any projects using ``wepy`` in the
:doc:`Introduction <introduction>`.

Just make a PR or issue to add your own.

Contacting the developers
-------------------------

You can contact developers directly via their contact info on the
`Dickson Lab website <https://www.egr.msu.edu/~alexrd/>`__, using the
`issue tracker <https://github.com/ADicksonLab/wepy/issues>`__, or on
`gitter.im <https://gitter.im/wepy/general>`__ chat.
