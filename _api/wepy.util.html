wepy.util package¶

Miscellaneous functions used in wepy that don’t fit elsewhere.

Warning

Deprecation warning!

Try not to rely on these functions as part of your stable code. These were included here in wepy because other libraries do not provide this functionality, although they probably should. That is these functions are outside of the purview of the goals of wepy and are not guaranteed to be included in wepy indefinitely.

Please do not rely on anything in the util module to exist indefinitely.

Any function is subject to deprecation at any release.

If you use these functions consider copying the source code.

If any of these functions or classes become reified and officially part of wepy they will go in a sub-module dedicated to their purposes.

But these will likely (and should) be moved to other libraries as they are more general purpose than wepy.
